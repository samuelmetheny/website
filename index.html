About Us

At Listen With Headphones, we provide creative and industry standard pre and post production services in Rap/Hip Hop. We offer services including: Mixing, mastering, recording, editing, beats, loops, drumkits, and collabs.


At Listen With Headphones, our goal is to collaborate with you to bring your vision to reality. With our extensive music knowledge, we're here to assist with everything from song completion to creation. For the highest quality Rap/Hip-Hop pre and post production services, as well as unique and creative beats, loops, and drum samples.
